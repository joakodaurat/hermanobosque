Hermano Bosque is an electro-acoustic music project
created by Manuel Pelliza (live loops & electronics) and Federico Giraudo (chromatic / midi harmonica & synths),
two argentinian artists living in the Mexican Caribbean.

MUSIC

Hermano Bosque's music is an ever-emerging journey through the here and now, where rhythm and movement guide a living experience in constant wonder and discovery. Their live sets are composed in real time influenced by organic, ambient and world sounds integrated to electronics and live looping techniques, resulting in bold and unique live sets every time they play. Since they started in 2016, all their sets were recorded live and they performed in many cities of Mexico, Argentina, Spain, Serbia, Marruecos and the United States.
