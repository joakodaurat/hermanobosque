WE ARE

Hermano Bosque's live sets are an ever-emerging journey through sound, where rhythm and movement guide a living experience of collective discovery. They are influenced by world music, organic and ambient sounds integrated to electronics and live looping techniques. Their music is composed in real time, resulting in bold and unique live sets every time they play. Since they started in 2016, all of their sets were recorded live and they have already performed in many cities of Mexico, Argentina, Spain, Serbia, Marruecos and the United States..
