Hermano Bosque is an electro-acoustic music project
created by Manuel Pelliza (live loops & electronics) and Federico Giraudo (chromatic / midi harmonica & synths),
two argentinian artists living in the Mexican Caribbean.

MUSIC

Hermano Bosque's live sets are an ever-emerging journey through sound, where rhythm and movement guide a living experience of collective discovery. Their music is composed in real time influenced by organic, ambient and world sounds integrated to electronics and live looping techniques, resulting in bold and unique live sets every time they play. Since they started in 2016, all of their sets were recorded live and they have already performed in many cities of Mexico, Argentina, Spain, Serbia, Marruecos and the United States..
